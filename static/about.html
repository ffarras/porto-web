Why UI/UX design?

Age 11 is my first fascination towards technology and building something, when I built a simple Folkative-like online media website using Blogger. This fascination fueled my journey to becoming a Computer Science student, where I discovered my new interest towards crafting design. It was during a Human-Computer Interaction course that I realized how my interest in crafting design could intersect with technology, thus settling myself to delve deeper into the realm of UI/UX design.

Now, I have involved in multiple projects working as both a designer and engineer, ranging from mobile apps to web dashboards. I cherish the opportunities to create delightful experiences that impact lots of people. Being a designer with an engineering background helps to understand problems more thoroughly, and also prevents me from heated feuds with developers over why the cards section has different paddings.

In my free time, I watch western movies and TV series and write reviews about them. Some of my proudest and longest reviews are about Succession, Yuni, and Seperti Dendam, Rindu Harus Dibayar Tuntas. You can read the rest on my Letterboxd (and connect with me if you have one!)
